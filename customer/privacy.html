Buds App - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December , 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how BudsApp Technologies ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Buds App mobile application (the "App"). Buds App is a productivity-focused super app offering features such as parcel delivery, on-demand services, and e-commerce.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information when you use the App:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Photos or videos (for example, as proof of parcel pickup or delivery, or to support specific on-demand services)

This information is primarily used for account creation, verification, authentication, notifications, and to complete orders or service requests placed through the App.

1.2 Location Information

The App uses your GPS location to enable features such as:

  • Determining parcel pickup and drop-off points
  • Assigning nearby service providers
  • Improving the accuracy of on-demand services

Location data is used to provide core functionality of the App. We do not use location data for unrelated purposes such as selling location data to third parties.

1.3 Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

As of now, we do not intentionally collect:

  • Payment card details (credit/debit card numbers, CVV, etc.)
  • Device identifiers for our own databases
  • IP addresses in our own systems

Please note that some of the third-party services we use (such as Firebase or Google services) may automatically collect device and usage-related information as part of their normal operations. Their data practices are described in their respective privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To provide and operate parcel delivery and on-demand services
  • To send service-related notifications (e.g., order updates)
  • To authenticate users (via email, phone, or third-party sign-in)
  • To support third-party integrations (e.g., Google services)
  • To monitor and fix errors and improve App reliability

If we enable analytics in the future (such as Google Analytics for Firebase), we may also use anonymized or aggregated data to understand how users interact with the App and improve our features and performance.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate certain features of the App. These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. The key third-party services we currently use include:

  • Firebase Authentication (user sign-in and auth)
  • Google Authentication (Google Sign-In)
  • Google Maps Platform (maps, routing, location)
  • Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics (may be enabled in the future for usage analytics)

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers to understand how they handle data:

  • Google & Firebase Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third parties.

We may share information only in the following limited situations:

  • With service providers that help us operate the App (e.g., Firebase, Google services), strictly for the purpose of providing our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or requests from authorities, where required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

We store data using:

  • Firebase (for authentication and application data)
  • AWS secured servers (for backend services and storage)

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including restricted access, secure communication channels, and security best practices on our cloud infrastructure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. We may retain certain information for a longer period where required by law, to resolve disputes, or to enforce our agreements.
