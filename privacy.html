Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Buds App ("Buds App", "we", "us", or "our") operates a multi-service delivery and business directory platform, including parcel delivery, food delivery, pharmacy delivery, grocery delivery, ecommerce ordering, and the Buds List business directory (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By creating an account or using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, profile photo, and password.
  • Location information: your delivery address and, with your permission, your device's real-time GPS location, used to fulfil deliveries, calculate fees, show nearby drivers, and power "near me" search in Buds List.
  • Order and transaction information: items ordered, delivery instructions, order history, and payment confirmation details. Card and bank details themselves are processed by our third-party payment processor and are not stored on our servers.
  • Buds List listing information: if you register a business listing, the business name, description, category/subcategory, logo, feature image, address, phone, website, and any other details you choose to submit for public display.
  • Communications: messages sent through in-app chat (e.g. between a customer and a Buds List business, or between a customer and a driver), reviews and ratings you submit, and support requests.
  • Device and usage information: device type, operating system, app version, push-notification token, crash logs, and general usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and provide the Services you request.
  • To process and fulfil orders and deliveries, including sharing necessary details with the assigned driver or vendor.
  • To operate the Buds List directory, including displaying listing information publicly to other users and enabling customers to contact or review a listed business.
  • To send order updates, delivery status, and service-related push notifications, emails, or SMS.
  • To send promotional or marketing communications, which you may opt out of at any time.
  • To provide customer support and respond to enquiries.
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms & Conditions.
  • To improve and maintain the reliability of the app through analytics and crash reporting.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With drivers and vendors, to the extent necessary to complete an order (e.g. your name, delivery address, and phone number).
  • With other users, for Buds List, a business listing's submitted details are shown publicly by design, and messages/reviews you post are visible to the relevant business or other users as applicable.
  • With service providers who help us operate the Services, such as cloud hosting, push notification delivery (e.g. Firebase/Google), maps and geolocation, and payment processing. These providers may only use your information to perform services on our behalf.
  • For legal reasons, if required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Buds App, our users, or the public.
  • In a business transfer, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to standard confidentiality arrangements.

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information; however, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You may access, update, or correct your account information at any time from within the app.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated personal information by contacting us, subject to any information we are required to retain for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes.
  • You may withdraw consent to location access at any time through your device settings, though this may limit or disable core features (e.g. delivery tracking, nearby search).
  • You may opt out of marketing communications using the unsubscribe link in emails or the notification settings in the app.

As we operate in Ontario, Canada, we handle personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy legislation.

6. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date above, and, where appropriate, communicated through the app.
